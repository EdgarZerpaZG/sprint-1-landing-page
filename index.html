A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite
websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your
favorite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices
so you can access them on the go

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our
simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete
control over how you manage your favorite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerfull search feature will help you find saved
sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of
your bookmarks.

Shres your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with
other. Create a shareable link that you can send at
the click of a button.
