A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite
websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your
favorite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices
so you can access them on the go

  • Simple Bookmarking
  • Speedy Searching
  • Easy Sharing

Bookmark in one click

control over how you manage your favorite sites.
